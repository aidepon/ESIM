How to play

Et Sic I Mori is a rpg game where you are trapped in a series of rooms, and your goal is to explore each room and uncover the secrets in each one. Proceed with caution as one wrong move will pull you back, and keep in mind that you must discover who and why you are. This game is an exploration of self, of memory, and future.

This project is based loosely on the five stages of grief and a mixture of self exploration borne out of the daydreams of a wandering mind.
